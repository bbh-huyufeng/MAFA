MAFA

Multi-Humanoid Cooperative Extreme Traction via Adaptive Force-Aware Learning

Project URL: https://bbh-huyufeng.github.io/MAFA/

Overview

MAFA studies force-aware humanoid traction with a reusable pulling skill and a compact coordinator for multi-humanoid teamwork.

Adaptive Traction Skill

A single-humanoid policy learns force-aware posture and stepping control for sustained high-load pulling.

Compact Team Coordination

A deployable coordinator converts individual pulling capability into synchronized multi-humanoid traction.

Real-Robot Validation

The approach is demonstrated on Tienkung 3.0 humanoids in both single-robot and cooperative pulling tests.

Two-Stage AMP

Expert mocap clips are organized around a two-stage AMP design that separates traction preparation from engaged backward pulling.
