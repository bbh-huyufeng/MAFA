Overview

MAFA studies force-aware humanoid traction with a reusable pulling skill and a compact coordinator for multi-humanoid teamwork.

Force-Aware Traction

A humanoid traction skill adapts body posture and stepping behavior under strong physical interaction.

Hierarchical Cooperation

A high-level policy coordinates multiple humanoids through a compact command interface.

Hardware-Oriented Design

The system is organized around deployable observations and real robot demonstration workflows.
